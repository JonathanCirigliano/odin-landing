Some information about panda

The panda is a bear species, it is characterised by its bold black and white coat and rotund body.

Pandas are endemic in China, mainly in Sichuan, and also in neighbouring Shaanxi and Gansu.

Pandas belong to the order Carnivora, the giant panda is folivore, with bamboo shoots and leaves making 99% of his diet.

The giant panda has often served as China's national symbol, appeared on Chinese Gold Panda coins since 1982.
